What is your purpose in life?


My purpose in life is to live as freely as possible, while also being a loving daughter, supportive friend, and responsible citizen. I strive to use my abilities and skills to help others, just as I have been helped by those around me. Whether through my career, relationships, or personal growth, my goal is to live as freely and authenticaly, making a positive impact on those around me, no matter how big or small.

~by Donna Mae Remulta~
~from BSIS II-B~
